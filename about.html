ABOUT ME

Learn more about my journey, design approach, and me—beyond the screen!

Hey, Priyanka here!

I’m a curious designer who loves turning complex ideas into clean, intuitive experiences—where user joy meets business success.

My journey has been anything but linear. I began by pursuing a postgraduate degree in Computer Applications, where I first discovered a fascination with interface design. This newfound interest led me to take a diploma in multimedia, allowing me to combine my technical skills with a love for creative expression. My career began as a web designer, crafting seamless experiences for the digital world. Over time, I transitioned into UX/UI/Product design, drawing on my background in front-end development to design products that are as functional as they are delightful.

I believe design is a collaborative effort. By combining empathy, precision, and teamwork, I aim to deliver designs that not only solve problems but also elevate experiences for the long term.

check my Resume

Design approach

1.Understand the "why" to ensure purpose-driven design.

2.Make decisions based on research, data, and testing—not bias.

3.Create solutions that delight users while driving business goals.

4.Embrace feedback and iteration to enhance the experience.

5.Design for real people, not just an idealized vision.

Outside work

I find inspiration in my garden, where nurturing plants teaches me growth and patience. I also love experimenting in the kitchen, playing strategic games, exploring new places, and indulging in great food.

Curious about the story behind my logo?

It represents the initials "PP" — one "P" from my native language and the other from English. This combination symbolizes the diversity that shapes my journey and creative approach.
