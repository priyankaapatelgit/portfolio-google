Key Features

02
  • Homepage Design
    • Created an impactful homepage with a strong visual identity, showcasing the agency's innovative and creative approach.
    • Added featured projects in an intuitive layout, allowing users to explore their work upfront.
  • Service Page

    Developed detailed pages showcasing the agency’s core services, including digital marketing, branding, social and web.

  • Responsive Design

    Optimized the website for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices, ensuring a seamless browsing experience for all users.

    Designed a visually engaging portfolio section to highlight completed projects, presenting challenges and outcomes in a distinctive and compelling way.

  • Contact and Inquiry Features

    Integrated a contact form and clickable contact details for easy inquiries.

  • Custom Branding
    • Developed a cohesive visual language aligned with the agency’s branding, including typography, color schemes, and iconography.
    • Ensured branding consistency across all pages for a professional and polished look.
