Who is Washing Well?

Washing Well Laundromat, is a trusted and customer-focused laundry service. Known for its hygienic and friendly environment, offering convenience and accessibility to customers at any time.

My Contribution:

Designed a responsive, user-friendly website for a local laundromat to showcase their services, cleanliness, and commitment to delivering a customer-focused experience.

Role:

Web Designer

Duration:

4 weeks

Team:

Solo Project

S/W Used:

HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Photoshop, Google Suites

Design Process

01
  • Wireframes & Mockups

    Created wireframes and high-fidelity mockups to ensure an intuitive layout and smooth navigation.

  • User-Centered Approach

    Focused on delivering a clean, modern aesthetic aligned with the laundromat's brand values.

Final Design

02
  • Responsive Layout

    Ensured the website adapts seamlessly across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

  • Key Features
    • Service descriptions with images.
    • A contact form for easy access.
    • Prominent uptime display
    • easily accessible location, phone number, email, and social links

Impact and Reflection

03

  • 01

    Designing for Impact

    • Enhanced online visibility and professionalism for the laundromat.
    • Increased customer engagement, leading to a 25% rise in service inquiries within the first month post-launch.

  • 02

    Empowering Local Businesses

    This project reinforced the importance of tailoring design to local business needs while ensuring accessibility and responsiveness. It highlighted the impact of thoughtful design in driving customer engagement for small businesses.
