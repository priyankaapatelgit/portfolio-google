Who is Power Punch?

Power Punch is an online platform that simplifies the process of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. It allows users to order fresh, nutritious juices tailored to their daily needs, ensuring their daily dose of energy and health is just a click away.

My Contribution:

As a freelance UX/UI designer, I led the end-to-end design process, including research, strategy, and delivery. My contributions included conducting user research to understand pain points and preferences, designing intuitive wireframes, prototypes, and visuals that aligned with the brand, and creating a scalable design system to ensure consistency.

Project Overview

01

  • PROBLEM

    In today’s fast-paced world, people often struggle to maintain a healthy diet due to their busy schedules. Junk food becomes a convenient choice, leading to poor nutrition and long-term health issues such as stress, fatigue, obesity, and high blood pressure. Despite the known benefits of fruits and vegetables in maintaining good health, the lack of accessible, healthy options poses a challenge.

  • SOLUTION

    The objective of Power Punch is to offer a seamless solution for busy individuals to meet their nutritional needs. The platform enables users to:

    • Access detailed product information, including benefits and ingredients.
    • Choose juices based on their specific health requirements.
    • Place daily or weekly orders effortlessly.
    • Receive fresh juice delivered directly to their doorstep, promoting a healthier lifestyle.

Design Stretagy

02
  • Executive Intent

    This project is all about making users order their desired fresh juice. Users can browse and select the organic, fresh, and cold-pressed juice as per their choice. In addition to that, they can find product details, benefits, and ingredients for each juice bottle. Users can also read dietary and our juice-related questions.

  • Target Audience

    Professionals, students, housewives, those persons who are interested to make their life full of nutrition.

  • General Tasks

    Professionals, students, housewives, those persons who are interested to make their life full of nutrition.

  • Technology Constraints

    As of now, there are no technology constraints. In small-town we can have some network issue.

  • Cross Channels

    All platforms: IOS, Android, Web

  • Marketing/ Branding Goals

    Increase profits: To increase the sale of juice bottles and maintain a retention rate Leverage Social: People won't share content unless we will ask them so we should develop shareable content like graphics, infographics, and quotes. Publishing Content: To increase our credibility we can write an article, share videos Use referrals: We can use a referral program to increase brand awareness

  • Critical Success Factor

    There are so many platforms from where users can buy juice bottles but our goal is to provide users fresh, organic, and cold-pressed juice with all nutrition at their doorstep.

  • Major Competitors

    Suja, Jamba, Pressed Juicery
