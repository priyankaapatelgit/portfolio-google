Who is Andaaz?

Andaaz Manufacturing caters to the hospitality industry’s demand for high-end, factory-direct furniture solutions. Founded by industry experts, Andaaz partners directly with procurement organizations and design firms, eliminating third-party layers to ensure transparency and tailored solutions. With trusted partnerships with select factories since inception, Andaaz delivers quality and customization to meet the unique needs of each establishment.

My Contribution:

Designed a user-friendly website for a hotel furniture manufacturing company to showcase their innovative designs, high-quality craftsmanship, and dedication to transforming creative concepts into functional furniture.

Role:

Web Designer

Duration:

6 weeks

Team:

2 Persons Team

S/W Used:

HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Photoshop, Google Suites

Design Process

01
  • Wireframes & Mockups

    Created wireframes and high-fidelity mockups to ensure an intuitive layout and smooth navigation.

  • User-Centered Approach

    Focused on delivering a clean, modern aesthetic aligned with the laundromat's brand values.

Key Features

02
  • Homepage Design

    Created a visually appealing and professional homepage emphasizing the brand’s commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability.

  • Product Showcase

    Developed an organized and visually rich product gallery to display various furniture categories like seating and lighting.

  • Client Projects

    Added a "Projects" section to feature completed hotel projects, demonstrating the brand's capabilities in delivering tailored, eco-friendly solutions.

  • Responsive Design

    Ensured seamless browsing across all devices, including mobile, tablet, and desktop, with optimized layouts for varying screen sizes.

  • User-Centric Navigation

    Designed intuitive navigation with clearly labeled menus, enabling users to easily explore services, products, and contact information.

  • Storytelling through Design
    • Highlighted the company’s journey, values, and unique selling points through an "About Us" section with compelling content.
    • Emphasized the company’s commitment to sustainable practices, eco-friendly materials, and ethical manufacturing processes.
  • Contact and Inquiry Features

    Integrated a contact form for inquiries, along with clickable phone numbers, email addresses, and a live chat option for quick communication.

  • Process and Sustainability
    • Detailed the end-to-end manufacturing process, from concept design to production, focusing on precision and quality.
    • Showcased sustainability initiatives, such as sourcing renewable materials, minimizing waste, and working with environmentally conscious factories.

Impact and Reflection

03

  • 01

    Enhanced Brand Visibility

    Boosted online visibility and professionalism for the brand, leading to increased inquiries from hotel chains and interior designers.

  • 02

    Increased Product Engagement

    Improved user engagement with the product gallery, driving a 30% rise in product-related inquiries within the first month post-launch.
